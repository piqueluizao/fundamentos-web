Harry Potter movies

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001)

Harry Potter is an orphan boy who lives unhappily with his uncles, the Dursleys. He receives a letter containing an invitation to join Hogwarts, a famous school specializing in training young wizards. Initially, Harry is prevented from reading the letter by his uncle, but he is soon visited by Hagrid, the Hogwarts gamekeeper, who arrives to take him to the school. Harry enters a magical world he never imagined, living several adventures with his new friends, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (2002)

After a miserable vacation at his aunt and uncle's house, Harry Potter prepares to return to Hogwarts and begin his second year at wizarding school. The day before school starts, the strange creature Dobby appears in his room and warns him that coming back is a mistake and that something very bad could happen if Harry insists on continuing his studies of witchcraft. The boy, however, is willing to take the risk and get rid of the troubled home.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)

It's the beginning of the third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft. Harry, Ron and Hermione have a lot to learn. But a threat haunts the school and it's called Sirius Black. After twelve years incarcerated in Azkaban Prison, he manages to escape and returns to avenge his master, Lord Voldemort. To make matters worse, the Dementors, guards supposedly sent to protect Hogwarts and follow in Black's footsteps, appear to be even more dangerous threats.

Harry Potter and the Globet of Fire (2005)

Harry returns for his fourth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, along with his friends Ron and Hermione. This time, there is a tournament between the three major schools of magic, with one participant selected from each school, by the Goblet of Fire. Harry's name pops up, even though he hasn't applied, and he has to compete.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2007)

After a disastrous summer, Harry returns to his fifth year at Hogwarts, one of the most difficult he will have to face. Very few students and parents believe him or Dumbledore about Voldemort's return, and an endless series of articles circulates saying that they are completely nuts. To top it off, Dolores Umbridge, the new Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher, proves to be the most disgusting person Harry has ever met.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2009)

In Harry's sixth year at Hogwarts, Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters are creating terror in the wizarding and muggle worlds. Dumbledore convinces his old friend Horace Slughorn to return to Hogwarts as a potions teacher after Harry finds a strange schoolbook. Draco Malfoy strives to carry out an action intended by Voldemort, while Dumbledore and Harry secretly work together to discover the method to destroy the Dark Lord once and for all.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 (2010)

Without the guidance and protection of their teachers, Harry, Ron and Hermione embark on a quest to destroy the Horcruxes, which are the sources of Voldemort's immortality. Though they must trust each other more than ever, dark forces threaten to tear them apart. Voldemort's Death Eaters have taken control of the Ministry of Magic and Hogwarts, and they are looking for Harry as he and his friends prepare for the final showdown.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2 (2011)

The clash between good and evil awaits young Harry, who prepares alongside Ron and Hermione for the final battle against Lord Voldemort. Harry turned into a boy of steel on a quest to rid the world of evil. The friends must search for the last Horcruxes that keep the cowardly sorcerer immortal.
